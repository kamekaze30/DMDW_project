## QUICK REFERENCE GUIDE
## ============================================

HEALTHY INDICATORS:
- Glucose: 70-100 mg/dL
- BMI: 18.5-24.9
- Blood Pressure: <80 mmHg
- Age: <40 years
- Diabetes Pedigree: <0.5

PRE-DIABETIC INDICATORS:
- Glucose: 100-125 mg/dL
- BMI: 25-29.9
- Blood Pressure: 80-89 mmHg
- Age: 40-50 years
- Diabetes Pedigree: 0.5-1.0

DIABETIC INDICATORS:
- Glucose: ≥126 mg/dL
- BMI: ≥30
- Blood Pressure: ≥90 mmHg
- Age: >50 years
- Diabetes Pedigree: >1.0

---

## HOW TO USE THESE TEST CASES
## ============================================

1. Open the MedPredict AI Dashboard
2. Click on "Risk Assessment" tab
3. Enter the values from any test case above
4. Click "Analyze Risk" button
5. Compare the predicted result with the expected result
6. Review the recommendations provided

---

## INTERPRETING RESULTS
## ============================================

✅ HEALTHY (Glucose < 100 mg/dL)
   - Green color scheme
   - Healthy probability displayed
   - Prevention guidelines shown

⚡ PRE-DIABETIC (Glucose 100-125 mg/dL)
   - Yellow/Orange color scheme
   - Warning messages
   - Action plan for lifestyle changes

⚠️ DIABETIC (Glucose ≥ 126 mg/dL)
   - Red color scheme
   - Critical alerts
   - Immediate medical consultation recommended

---

## NOTES FOR TESTING
## ============================================

- The model considers ALL 8 features together, not just glucose
- Age and BMI significantly impact the risk calculation
- Family history (Diabetes Pedigree) plays an important role
- Multiple pregnancies can increase risk for women
- Results are probability-based and for educational purposes only
- Always consult a healthcare professional for medical decisions
